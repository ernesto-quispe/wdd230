Santiago Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Overview

Site Name: Santiago Chamber of Commerce

I live in Santiago and it's the name of the chamber of commerce.

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide a platform for the business members of the chamber to communicate who they are, what they do, and news and events sponsored by the business members. It will also provide a way for prospective members to request membership in the chamber of commerce.

Scenarios

  • What are the requirements for becoming a member of the Santiago Chamber of Commerce?
  • What community events are scheduled for this month?
  • How do I contact the chamber's members or directors?
  • What are the benefits of becoming a member of the chamber of commerce?
