Who We Are

Welcome to Scoots! Your gateway to the thrilling, uncharted territories of Cozumel! We’re not just a rental company, we’re your ticket to an unforgettable island adventure!

Our journey began with our fearless founder, Carlos Mendez, a Cozumel native with a burning desire to share the island’s hidden treasures. Tired of seeing visitors miss out on the true beauty of Cozumel, Carlos took matters into his own hands. He created Scoots, a company that breaks away from the usual, offering you the freedom to explore the island’s secret spots on your own terms.

At Scoots, we’re all about the thrill of discovery! We offer top-notch scooters, jeeps, and ATVs, perfect for adventurers ready to veer off the beaten path. We believe in the magic of Cozumel, and we’re here to help you uncover it!

So, are you ready to escape the crowds? Ready to discover the island’s best-kept secrets? With Scoots, you’re not just renting a vehicle, you’re embarking on the adventure of a lifetime!

Join us at Scoots, and let’s make your Cozumel experience truly unforgettable!
