Applicant Information

Membership Level

Non Profit:

Free option for non profit organizations.

Benefits include:

  • Company listed as a non profit.

Bronze Level:

A yearly fee of $50.00

Benefits include:

  • Spotlight placement at least once a month.
  • Access to monthly networking events.
  • Listing in the online member directory.

Silver Level:

A yearly fee of $80.00

Benefits include:

  • Spotlight placement at least 3 times a month.
  • Access to monthly networking events and quarterly workshops.
  • Listing in the online member directory with a highlighted profile.
  • One complimentary ticket to the annual Chamber gala.

Gold Level:

A yearly fee of $150.00

Benefits include:

  • Spotlight placement at least once a week.
  • Access to all networking events, workshops, and exclusive Gold member events.
  • Premium listing in the online member directory with a highlighted and featured profile.
  • Two complimentary tickets to the annual Chamber gala.
  • Opportunity to host a networking event.
