WHO WE ARE

Welcome to the Santiago Metropolitan Chamber of Commerce, where we unite over 2,000 diverse businesses from the vibrant heart of Chile. Established in 1919, our chamber stands as a beacon of support and growth for enterprises large and small. We are dedicated to fostering a thriving economic environment by advocating for the interests of our members and facilitating a dynamic exchange of information and services. Our extensive network, including 30 sister chambers across the nation, empowers us to champion the cause of commerce with a forward-thinking approach and state-of-the-art technology. Ripley

Ripley is a prominent Chilean department store chain that offers a wide range of products, including technology, fashion, and home goods. Founded in 1956, it has expanded its presence to Peru and operates a successful online platform.

Cencosud

Cencosud, now known as Cenco, is a retail powerhouse in Latin America, operating a variety of supermarkets, home improvement stores, and shopping centers. With its roots in Chile, Cencosud has grown to serve several countries, providing customers with quality products and services.
